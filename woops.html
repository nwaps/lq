You're Inside Low Quality

Q: What is #low-quality?

A: #low-quality is a designated space for users whose behavior disrupts #general

Reasons for being placed here INCLUDE but are NOT limited to:

  • Being a consistent nuisance
  • Harassing other users
  • Having a suspicious Discord account (No profile picture, random usernames, etc)
  • Spamming messages or images excessively
  • Posting incoherent or nonsensical content
  • Repeated low-effort or bait posts
  • Needing behavioral improvement as determined by moderation
